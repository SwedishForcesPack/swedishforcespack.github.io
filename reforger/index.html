Swedish Forces Pack

Description

The original Swedish Armed Forces experience for ArmA are now entering the next generation with the first release for Arma Reforger.

SFP is now leaving the previous games and relases behind and continue with 100% new content made for Reforger with professional tools with the highest quality.
Focus is infantry and the era (1988-1992) represented in Reforger.

Our first release comes with a Conflict version and a coop in order to test our mod, but more missions are planned, both cooperative and competitive with the hopes to bring players from ArmA3 to Reforger as a preperation for ArmA4.

Features

Uniforms and soldier equipment

  • M59 Uniform, jacket/pants, boots
  • Helmet m37 (clean + camo variants)
  • Combat belt 304 (with canteen, mag pouches)
  • LK 35 (backpack)

  • Compass type 3
  • Flashlight 3
  • Firstaid kit
  • Binocular 6x30

    • Weapons

    Anti-tank

    • Pansarskott m/86 - the AT4 84mm disposable single-shot recoilless anti-tank weapon.

    Grenades

    • Shgr 56 - a Swedish fragmentation grenade with a 3.5 second fuse.

    Pistols

    • Pistol 07 - the Swedish licensed production of the FN M1903.
    • Signalpistol 50 - a Swedish signal and illumination flare gun.

    Rifles

    • Ak4 - the iconic H&K G3A3 modified for Swedish use. The versions include with M203 attachment and marksman edition..
    • Ak5 - the FN FNC, standard version and the Bertil (Marksman with SUSAT optics)
    • Kpist m/45 - the "Swedish K", a fully automatic submachinegun used by the Swedish Armed Forces between 1945 and 2007.
    • KSP 58B - the FN MAG.
