Arma 3

Swedish Forces Pack

The Swedish Forces Pack for Arma 3 features more than 30 different sea, air, and land vehicles, as well as a large assortment of weapons ranging from rifles, pistols, and disposable AT-launchers to heavy SAM systems and artillery guns.
Also part of the package are modules for Anti-submarine Warfare, setting Unit Insignia and vehicle ID, as well as spawners for cluster bombs and anti-ship missiles for use by a Zeus game master.
Everything is of course compatible with CBA A3, ACE3, ALiVE, and your favourite radio addon.
Also available are JSRS compatibility addons for that extra immersive sound.

Sturkö

Sturkö is an island in Blekinge, near one of Swedens largest naval bases, Karlskrona.
On the 27 October 1981 at approximatly 20.00, a Soviet submarine ran aground on a skerry in Gåsefjärden, directly east of Sturkö and just 10km from Karlskrona.
The Soviets sent a "rescue" party while the Swedish prime minister Thorbjörn Fälldin gave the order to "Hold the border", instructing the Swedish Armed Forces to shoot if any Soviet ships crossed into Swedish waters.
Eventually, after a few days, the submarine was salvaged and handed over to the Soviets in international waters, effectively ending the crisis.
